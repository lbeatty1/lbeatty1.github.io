I am an environmental and energy economist at the Environmental Defense Fund (EDF), where I study policy options to reduce emissions from oil and gas production. Previously, I was a Highmeadows Postdoctoral Fellow at EDF. I received my PhD in Agricultural and Resource Economics from the University of Maryland, where I was an NBER Pre-Doctoral Fellow in Energy Economics.
